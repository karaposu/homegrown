# Innovation: A Better Name Than "Telemetry"

## Seed

"Telemetry" is too generic — means "data from a remote source." Doesn't convey quality measurements about the discipline's own run.

## Surviving Candidates

| Candidate | Explicit? | No evaluation? | Self-explanatory? | Wears well? |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Run Metrics** | Very | Yes | Yes | Yes — invisible after 50 uses |
| **Run Stats** | Very | Yes | Yes | Yes — casual but clear |
| **Readout** | Moderate | Yes | Partial | Yes — short and clean |

## Winner: Run Metrics

Says exactly what it is. No metaphor. No ambiguity. Two words. Self-explanatory to anyone.

For sensemaking: "Saturation Indicators (Run Metrics)" — preserves domain keyword.
