# Structural Critique: The Comprehend Discipline Design

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | Success criteria |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| Distinctness | CRITICAL | Unique transform, unique core operation, no overlap |
| Internal coherence | CRITICAL | Parts form consistent system without contradiction |
| Operational clarity | HIGH | Clear entry, activities, outputs, exit tests |
| System coherence | HIGH | Clean interfaces, no overlap confusion |
| Minimality | MEDIUM | Every component earns its place |
| Robustness | MEDIUM | Works across domains, for AI and human |

## Candidate Verdicts

### 1. The 5-Phase Process — SURVIVE

**Prosecution:** Reverse engineering methodology with new names. Dynamic phase doesn't generalize beyond code.

**Defense:** Every discipline imports. Integration of 6 innovations from 7+ domains is the value. No phase is removable. Aspects model handles non-code operationalization.

**Verdict:** SURVIVE. Caveats: review phase names for domain-neutrality; add scale-down guidance for quick tasks.

---

### 2. The 4 Comprehension Aspects — REFINE

**Prosecution:** Contextual IS exploration. Temporal IS reflection. Adding aspects that overlap other disciplines undermines the distinctness argument that justified the discipline's existence. LOW confidence sensemaking resolution was treated as settled prematurely.

**Defense:** Intent is clearly distinct (design-space modeling). Developers blend aspects naturally. Contextual adds causal understanding Exploration doesn't; Temporal adds causal evolution Reflection doesn't.

**Collision:** Prosecution wins on Contextual and Temporal (overlap is real). Defense wins on Intent (genuinely distinct). Practical blending argument is valid.

**Verdict:** REFINE.
- **Mechanistic**: PRIMARY aspect (founding justification)
- **Intent**: PRIMARY aspect (genuinely distinct — design-space models)
- **Contextual**: EXTENDED mode (border with Exploration — acknowledge, don't claim)
- **Temporal**: EXTENDED mode (border with Reflection — acknowledge, don't claim)

---

### 3. Flexible Scaffold with Mandatory Checkpoints — SURVIVE

**Prosecution:** "Flexible but mandatory" is contradictory. Can't-perturb cases undermine the discipline's strongest claim.

**Defense:** Flexibility is in the path, not the destination. Tests are the invariant. Mental perturbation is legitimate (physics uses thought experiments). Other disciplines are also non-linear.

**Verdict:** SURVIVE. Caveat: add explicit testing hierarchy — execution-based (strongest) → scenario-based (moderate) → reasoning-based (weakest but valid).

---

### 4. The Depth Hierarchy — REFINE

**Prosecution:** "Mechanistic" is both an aspect name and a depth level name. Boundaries between levels are fuzzy. Generative is unreachable for most tasks.

**Defense:** Naming collision is real but fixable. Levels are defined by concrete tests. Generative is reachable for AI and is a valuable North Star.

**Verdict:** REFINE. Rename levels:
- Surface → **Descriptive**
- Structural → **Structural** (no change)
- Mechanistic → **Causal** (avoids aspect collision)
- Predictive → **Predictive** (no change)
- Generative → **Generative** (add guidance for when to target)

---

### 5. "One Process, Multiple Aspects" Unification — SURVIVE

**Prosecution:** The phases mean different things per aspect. If you need a table to explain each, you have four processes wearing one label.

**Defense:** Unification is at the functional layer (acquisition → verification → crystallization), which IS universal. Developers blend aspects naturally; unified scaffold enables this.

**Verdict:** SURVIVE. Caveat: define the process at the functional layer. Aspect-specific tables are examples, not definitions.

---

### 6. Verification as Unique Value — SURVIVE

**Prosecution:** Critique already does adversarial testing. Differential phase blurs acquisition/verification. Prediction testing may propagate to other disciplines.

**Defense:** Different subject (own model vs. candidates). Comprehension-through-testing is a genuine structural insight. Primary ≠ exclusive — other disciplines have observation too but only Exploration makes it core.

**Verdict:** SURVIVE. No caveats.
