## Phase 3 - Ambiguity Collapse

### Ambiguity 1: Strict vs liberal P1 family definition for cross-chain counting

**Strongest counter-interpretation:**
Strict reading. P1 was originally named in the first LOOP_DIAGNOSE finding as "Sensemaking Phase 1 axiom items adopted without testing." This chain's H-A is at the SV2-SV3 terminology layer, not Phase 1. Counting it as a P1 instance is liberal interpretation that may not survive the next chain. M6 promotion should wait until P1 has 3 strict instances.

**Why the counter-interpretation fails (structural grounds):**
The P1 family definition in the previous LOOP_DIAGNOSE finding was actually broader: *"Sensemaking adopting a load-bearing concept without Phase 3 testing."* The "Phase 1 axiom items" wording was descriptive of the first two chains' specific instances, not a strict definition. The corrected loop in this chain explicitly tested the "Route-Memory Preflight" terminology in Phase 3 / Ambiguity 1 — exactly the same Phase-3-test mechanism. So this chain's H-A is mechanistically the same family as chains 1 and 2: a load-bearing concept stabilized in Sensemaking output without being tested in Phase 3. The layer differs (Phase 1 vs SV2-SV3), but the family-defining property is the same.

**Confidence:** HIGH. The corrected loop's Phase 3 / Ambiguity 1 in this chain is structurally identical to the corrected loops' Phase 3 work in chains 1 and 2 — same mechanism (test the concept against its strongest counter-interpretation), different specific concept.

**Resolution:**
Adopt the liberal reading. P1 family definition: *"Sensemaking adopting a load-bearing concept without Phase 3 testing — at any layer where the concept stabilizes (Phase 1 / Constraints, Phase 1 / Foundational Principles, SV2+ terminology)."* Three chains × three layers × same mechanism = 3-chain threshold met.

**What is now fixed?**
P1 family covers all load-bearing concept stabilization in Sensemaking. M6 promotion is mechanically triggered.

**What is no longer allowed?**
Restricting P1 to Phase 1 axiom items only.

**What now depends on this choice?**
M6 promotion to ACTIONABLE; the unified anchor-and-terminology interrogation rule (M1+N2+O1).

**What changed in the conceptual model?**
The P1 family is layer-agnostic; the family-defining property is the mechanism, not the layer.

### Ambiguity 2: Are C-dim (Critique missing dimension) and C-pros (Critique weak prosecution) separable hypotheses?

**Strongest counter-interpretation:**
They are not separable. C-pros is a consequence of C-dim: with the missing dimension, prosecution had nowhere to go; the prosecution-strength weakness is downstream of the dimension articulation. C-pros should be folded into C-dim.

**Why the counter-interpretation fails (structural grounds):**
The corrected Critique's prosecution against Candidate A was constructed at the user-perspective level *"Calling this 'Route-Memory Preflight' risks creating another named mandatory operation. That is exactly the smell the user objected to: Navigation seems to grow side rituals."* — and this prosecution does not require the Conceptual cleanliness dimension to exist. Even with the prior's dimension list (Naturalness, Navigation unity, Explicitness, Anti-bloat, Automation readiness, Coherence), Navigation unity could have hosted the same prosecution: *"under Navigation unity, this candidate creates a new named operation in users' minds, which violates 'one Navigation discipline.'"* The prior Critique chose to construct only the dimension-level objection (*"Adds another preflight field"*) rather than the user-perspective-level objection. So C-pros has independent culpability in prosecution construction, separate from C-dim's missing axis.

**Confidence:** HIGH. The corrected Critique's prosecution can be re-constructed against the prior's dimension list without requiring the missing dimension; this demonstrates C-pros is independently observable.

**Resolution:**
C-dim and C-pros are separable hypotheses with different corrective levers. C-dim → O2 (add operation-parsimony dimension). C-pros → O3 (Critique prosecution-strength check that explicitly constructs user-perspective-level objections).

**What is now fixed?**
Two distinct Critique-level hypotheses; two distinct corrective candidates.

**What is no longer allowed?**
Folding C-pros into C-dim; treating C-pros as "just downstream of missing dimension."

**What now depends on this choice?**
The maintenance candidate set: O2 (operation-parsimony dimension) AND O3 (prosecution-strength check) are both proposed.

**What changed in the conceptual model?**
Critique has two surfaces (dimension list, prosecution construction) where the same family of failures can manifest with different corrective levers.
