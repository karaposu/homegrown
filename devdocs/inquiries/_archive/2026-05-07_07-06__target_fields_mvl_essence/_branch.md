# Branch: Target Fields And MVL Essence

## Question

Are the five Target Alignment fields fixed pre-loop inputs, or are they part of what MVL/MVL+ discovers and stabilizes through its thinking disciplines?

## Goal

A good answer should clarify whether `evidence_domain`, `diagnosis_target`, `maintenance_target`, `implementation_target`, and `out_of_scope_target` should be known before the loop starts, inferred during the loop, revised as understanding improves, or treated as the essence of what MVL does. The user should be able to decide how these fields should behave without accidentally turning MVL into a rigid form-fill before thinking happens.

## Scope Check

Question covers goal. It asks for the fields' lifecycle, their relationship to the MVL/MVL+ disciplines, and whether they are preconditions, outputs, or evolving state.
